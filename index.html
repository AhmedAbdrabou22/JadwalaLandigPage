overview :

Jadwal company is an Omani Company which aim to develop the event Management industry in Oman by providing a ticketing system for all kinds of Events, Activities and Workshops happing in Oman. Jadwal helps the Organizers to promote and sell an online ticket in seamless platform. The Organizers will be able to provide an easy experience to their audience by providing them with a platform where they can book and pay for their tickets online. The online Tickets will have a barcode with a seat number which will help the organizers to manage their events in professional way

Profile

Jadwal is an online platform for advertising and selling all kinds of events/workshops tickets in Oman. It has been noted that many events hosting organizations are facing difficulties in trying to reach the intended customers. Therefore, this platform will make it easier for these organizations to sell, and market their events to people. The platform will have two types of users. The first type is the individual users who are looking for activities/plans/shows/workshops such as; events for fun or workshops to improve their skills or even a show or hiking group. The second type of users are the organizations which are hosting or organizing events/workshops. The main income of this business is going to be from subscription. fees (a one-time fee the organization pays to join the service), Tickets commission (% of sold tickets) and could also be from Ads. In simpler terms, Jadwal is going to be the market for all events/workshops/activates/shows in Oman
